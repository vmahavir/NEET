📌 Notes as per FRA Minutes (12 Aug 2025):

  • Fee ceiling: The approved fee is the maximum that can be charged per student per year. It includes tuition, library, gymkhana, exam, development, laboratory, and other mandatory charges, but excludes hostel, mess, and student insurance.
  • No capitation / excess fee: Charging more than the approved fee is ‘profiteering’ (Section 2(s) of the Act) and liable for penalty up to twice the excess amount, plus refund to students.
  • Display requirement: Every college must display the approved fee on its notice board and website (Marathi & English; also in minority language if applicable).
  • One-year fee only: Institutions cannot collect fees for more than one academic year in advance.
  • Fee lock-in: The approved fee applies to all students admitted in A.Y. 2025-26 and cannot be revised during the course.
  • Students can file complaints on the FRA grievance portal if any institution charges above the approved fee.
