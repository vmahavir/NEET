❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the BAMS fee in government colleges in Maharashtra?

Ranges from ₹52,200 to ₹90,000 per year for state quota seats.

What is the lowest private BAMS fee in Maharashtra?

Around ₹1,23,000 per year at Sunil Ramsinh Chunawale Ayurved College, Chikhli.

What is the highest private BAMS fee?

Aditya Ayurved College, Beed has the highest at ₹3,30,000 per year.

Who decides private BAMS fees?

The Fees Regulating Authority (FRA), Maharashtra approves the fee structure annually.

Is hostel fee included in tuition?

No, hostel and mess charges are separate, typically ₹40,000–₹80,000 per year.

Is NEET mandatory for BAMS?

Yes, NEET UG is compulsory for all BAMS admissions in Maharashtra.

How many government BAMS colleges are in Maharashtra?

There are 8 government Ayurvedic colleges across the state.

How many private BAMS colleges are there?

Maharashtra has 85+ private Ayurvedic colleges approved by NCISM.

What is the NRI quota fee?

Approximately 3 to 5 times the institutional fee, varying by college.

Can students from other states apply?

Yes, through institutional and management quota seats in private colleges. State quota is mainly for Maharashtra domicile.
