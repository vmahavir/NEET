Admission Strategy

Do not depend on direct admission. NEET qualification and Maharashtra AYUSH counselling participation are required.

  1. Register for Maharashtra AYUSH CAP counselling
  2. Fill maximum possible BAMS college choices
  3. Do not stop after Round 1
  4. Participate in Round 2, Round 3 and Stray Vacancy Round
  5. Keep private college options open
