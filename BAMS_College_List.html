Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the annual fee structure for BAMS courses in Maharashtra AYUSH colleges?

The Maharashtra Ayush Colleges Fee Structure for BAMS ranges from ₹35,000 to ₹3,00,000 annually, depending on whether the college is government or private.

How much is the fee for BHMS courses under Maharashtra AYUSH colleges?

The Maharashtra Ayush Colleges Fee Structure for BHMS courses is ₹25,000 to ₹2,50,000 per year, with government colleges charging less and private colleges charging higher.

What is the fee for BUMS programs in Maharashtra AYUSH colleges?

According to the Maharashtra Ayush Colleges Fee Structure, the BUMS course fees range between ₹40,000 to ₹2,20,000 annually.

Do Maharashtra AYUSH colleges have different fees for management quota seats?

Yes, the Maharashtra Ayush Colleges Fee Structure for management quota seats is higher, usually ₹4,00,000 to ₹7,00,000 per year.

Are hostel charges included in the Maharashtra AYUSH colleges fee structure?

No, hostel charges are not included in the Maharashtra Ayush Colleges Fee Structure. Hostel fees usually cost ₹40,000 – ₹80,000 per year.
